Asteroid 2020 VT4

Asteroid 2020 VT4 made headlines when it passed extremely close to Earth on November 13, 2020. Discovered by the Asteroid Terrestrial-impact Last Alert System (ATLAS) just 15 hours after its closest approach, this small asteroid passed within just 400 kilometers (250 miles) of Earth's surface, even closer than many satellites in geostationary orbit. At an estimated size of 5 to 10 meters (16 to 33 feet), 2020 VT4 is comparable in size to a large car or small bus.
