Comet-13POlbers

Comet 13P/Olbers is a periodic comet that was discovered by German astronomer Heinrich Wilhelm Olbers in 1815. It has an orbital period of about 69 years, meaning it returns to the inner Solar System roughly every seven decades. 13P/Olbers last passed near the Sun in 1956 and is expected to return in 2024. During its last appearance, it was visible to the naked eye, and astronomers are eagerly awaiting its return to study how it has evolved over time.
